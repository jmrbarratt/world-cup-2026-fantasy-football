Entry Fee & Prize Money
The entry fee is £15 per team. 50% of all takings will be donated to the British Red Cross. Prize money is awarded to the top 4 places:
  • Winner: 20% of all takings
  • Runner-up: 15% of all takings
  • Third place: 10% of all takings
  • Fourth place: 5% of all takings

Note 1: Red Cards for Goalkeepers and Defenders
If a Goalkeeper or Defender receives a red card, he will continue to lose points for any goals conceded by his team thereafter. However, if he has played at least 60 minutes without conceding a goal and the team do not concede a goal after he is sent off, he will still receive clean sheet points.
Note 2: Knockout Stages
In the event of a draw at the end of normal time in the knockout stages, clean sheet points will be awarded at the end of normal time (players must have played at least 60 minutes).
Note 3: Penalty Shootouts
Goals scored in penalty shootouts DO NOT count towards a player's points tally.
